Biochemistry Research

Description

Brendan is currently working on a biochemistry research project with Dr. Myer and other students at Dixie State University. Specifically, they are looking at how the enzyme glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase reacts to different levels of glucose in the blood. Further more, they are investigating how the production of reducing agents (antioxidants) changes and if those changes can be mitigated by the addition of synthetic reducing agents (antioxidants).
